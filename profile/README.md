# kicktheclouds

> **Clear the sky. Stand on solid ground.**

Some weather isn't weather. The fog of cruelty, the haze of bad-faith noise, the storm clouds that roll in when hatred is allowed to call itself an opinion — it settles over public life and makes it hard to see, hard to breathe, hard to know where to stand. This is a place that refuses to treat that as just the climate. The clouds can be kicked.

The stance is plain: stand against hatred, insist on accountability, and turn principle into action rather than letting it evaporate into commentary. Not outrage for its own sake, and not vague good intentions, but the steady, unglamorous work of naming what's wrong, supporting the people who do right, and making it costly to do harm. Clear skies overhead; solid ground underfoot.

A small push against the weather, in the belief that enough small pushes change it.
